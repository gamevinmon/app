How it works

  • • VIN is an ERC20 token on Monad with a fixed total supply.
  • • Swap contract holds VIN/MON and enforces a 1:1 rate.
  • • Dice contract holds VIN as a reward pool (bankroll).
  • • Dev cannot withdraw VIN from swap or dice pools.
  • • All game outcomes are computed on-chain from block data.

100,000,000 VIN are permanently allocated: 50,000,000 VIN to the Swap Pool and 50,000,000 VIN to the Dice Reward Pool.

VIN Roadmap

Fixed supply. Two pools. Community-driven listing.

Phase 1 – 1:1 Swap (Current)

  • Total supply: 100,000,000 VIN.
  • 50,000,000 VIN locked in Swap Pool (VIN ⇄ MON 1:1).
  • 50,000,000 VIN locked in Dice Reward Pool.
  • Dev has no right to withdraw from both pools.
  • To get VIN from the Swap Pool: swap MON → VIN 1:1.
  • To get VIN from the Reward Pool: play Dice and win.
  • In this phase, VIN price in USD is derived from MON price on CoinGecko.

Phase 2 – Swap Pool Depleted

  • When the 50,000,000 VIN in the Swap Pool are fully swapped out, no more 1:1 VIN from MON.
  • VIN becomes harder to obtain: only through market or winning Dice.
  • The community may:
    • Request CEX listings for VIN.
    • Create VIN/MON liquidity pools on DEX.
  • Until VIN is listed externally, VIN price in USD is still pegged to MON price.

Phase 3 – External Listings

  • Once VIN is listed on DEX/CEX, VIN will have a market-driven price.
  • VIN price in USD will be taken from CoinGecko / CoinMarketCap.
  • VIN becomes an independent asset, no longer strictly tied to MON.
  • Holders can trade, provide liquidity, and build new products around VIN.
